GNAT Not Any Timer

GNAT is a project I have created solely for my own purpose and pleasure. It's a work in progress and continually serves two purposes:

  1. as my favorite productivity tool - Pomodoro!
  2. as a self-invented HTML5/CSS/JS self-learning course.

There's not much more to be said about this small piece of software.

Here's some of what I've taken from GNAT:
  • A much needed brush up on HTML, as my last encounter with HTML was sometime in the last millennium.
  • A crash course in the voodoo that is CSS.
  • Proof, not least to myself, that I can manage time in JS.
  • A starting point in web design to redesign and rebuild my portfolio.
